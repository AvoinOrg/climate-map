This layer shows the impact of improved forestry operations on the CO2e impact of forest's carbon balance over a 50 year period, compared to traditional forestry methods.
The calculation of the CO2 impact comes from extensively verified models developed by Arvometsä.

The data covers around 75% of Finland's forests. Most of the forest plots ("stands") shown are privately owned.
Norway's forests are also shown, but their CO2 saving potential has not been calculated yet.
